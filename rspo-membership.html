RSPO membership & sustainability.

Sustainable, traceable palm is the foundation of everything we ship — from refined oil to finished bar soap.

Our RSPO commitment

We are committed to sourcing palm oil produced to Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO) principles — supporting responsible production that protects forests, communities and livelihoods.

Certifications

Our operations and products are supported by recognised quality, sustainability and food-safety certifications, including RSPO, ISCC and Halal. Current certificates and licence details are available to customers on request.

Traceability

We maintain documentation that lets our customers stand behind the origin of the oil in every product — with a certificate of analysis and traceability records issued per lot.

Responsible operations

We work to run our refining and saponification efficiently, reducing waste, and to support the communities around our facilities.

Documentation

For certificates, membership details or audit documentation, contact info@indosultan.com.
