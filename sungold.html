Sungold is produced end-to-end at PT Indo Sultan Jaya — from saponified palm and palm-kernel noodles through to the stamped, fragranced and wrapped retail bar, for a consistent, hard, long-lasting bar across the range.

Driven by a commitment to quality, sustainability and innovation, Sungold is offered for private-label and distribution partners, with full container loadability and a certificate of analysis per lot. Detailed pack and loadability figures are confirmed on order.
