Clean frying performance, lot after lot.

Our palm olein is refined, bleached and deodorized in-house, then fractionated to the iodine value your application demands. Low free-fatty-acid content and a high smoke point give consistent fry life and a neutral taste profile.

Available unbranded for re-packers or filled under your own label across bottle, pouch, jerry-can and tin formats — backed by RSPO-certified, fully traceable feedstock.
