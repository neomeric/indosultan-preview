Consistent feedstock for downstream chemistry.

We fractionate and split refined palm oil into RBD stearin and olein, distilled fatty acids and refined glycerine — supplying the oleochemical, soap, candle, lubricant and biodiesel industries.

Each product is delivered to a tight specification window with full certificate of analysis and traceability documentation per lot.
