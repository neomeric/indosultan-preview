The contributions of medicated soap to global health.

Medicated and antibacterial bars play a real role in everyday hygiene — preventing infection and supporting public health worldwide.

Hygiene at scale

For many households, a simple medicated bar is a first line of defence — supporting handwashing routines that reduce the spread of common infections.

Reliable, affordable, well-formulated bars therefore have an outsized impact on community health, especially across the emerging markets we serve.

Our medicated range

We produce medicated bars under our own brands — Medix and Medivo — formulated to claim and to local regulation, and available for private label.
