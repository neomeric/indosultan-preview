About Us

A family house in the business of palm.

PT Indo Sultan Jaya is a vertically-integrated producer of cooking oil, palm fats, soap noodles and finished bar soap — built over a decade into a supplier that serves B2B buyers across five continents.

"Quality is the best business plan." We've built the entire company around that single idea.

PT Indo Sultan Jaya stands as a leader in the palm oil and derivatives industry, known for delivering premium-quality products and fostering long-term partnerships with both customers and suppliers.

Our state-of-the-art saponification plant, oil refinery and soap manufacturing facilities in Indonesia proudly serve markets across Asia, Africa, Europe, Oceania and the Americas — offering innovative, high-quality products that meet the demands of a diverse clientele.

Our journey

From a Jakarta trading desk to a five-continent producer.

2015

Founded in Bekasi

Shahid Sultan establishes PT Indo Sultan Jaya to trade refined palm products into South & Southeast Asia.

2017

Refinery & saponification plant

Commissioning of our own RBD refinery and saponification line in Cikarang, Bekasi — moving from trader to producer.

2020

Export into Africa & the Middle East

Soap noodle and bar-soap contracts open across 20+ new markets; Halal MUI and Kosher certification secured.

2023

Oleochemical derivatives

Fatty acids, stearin and glycerine lines added, serving cosmetic, biodiesel and personal-care formulators.

2025

50% stake in Sunindo "Vascali"

Strategic acquisition expands finished-goods capacity, with co-founder Ahmed Sultan driving the next chapter as CEO.
