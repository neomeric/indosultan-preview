A whiter base means a better bar.

Produced on our continuous saponification line from refined palm and palm-kernel oil, our noodles deliver high total fatty matter, low moisture and excellent colour — the foundation of a hard, long-lasting, low-mush bar.

Toilet grades span two palm-to-kernel ratios — TSN 702A and 680A on 80/20, TSN 702 and 689 on 90/10 — at 76–78% total oil matter, alongside Snow White (TFM 78). Laundry grades LSN 746, 646 and 600 plus translucent and multipurpose bases complete the range.
