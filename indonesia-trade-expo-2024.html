Successful participation at the 39th Indonesia Trade Expo.

IndoSultan exhibited at the 39th Trade Expo Indonesia (TEI 2024) in BSD City, Banten — meeting buyers from across our export markets.

On the floor

Held 9–12 October 2024, TEI is one of the region's largest B2B trade events. Our team showcased the full range — palm-oil derivatives, soap noodles and finished bar soap — to international buyers and distributors.

It was a chance to put product in people's hands, talk specifications, and start new partnerships face to face.

Why expos matter

For a B2B producer, nothing replaces meeting buyers directly: discussing grades, packaging and logistics in person shortens the path from first sample to first container.

Thank you to everyone who visited the booth — we look forward to continuing the conversations that started there.
