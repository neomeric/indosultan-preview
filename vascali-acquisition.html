PT Indo Sultan Jaya acquires 50% stake in Sunindo “Vascali”.

PT Indo Sultan Jaya has acquired a 50% stake in PT Sunindo Bangun Kersana — known by its “Vascali” brand — closing October 2024 with new finished-goods capacity and fresh ambitions.

A strategic step up

The acquisition broadens our finished-goods capacity and strengthens the consumer-products side of the business, alongside our established palm-derivatives, soap-noodle and bar-soap lines.

It also marks the next chapter for the family house, with co-founder Ahmed Sultan driving the company forward as CEO.

Quality is the best business plan.

What it means for partners

For private-label and export partners, the move means more capacity and a wider finished-goods range — backed by the same commitment to quality and traceability that defines everything we ship.

If you'd like to discuss volumes or new product opportunities off the back of this expansion, our trade desk is ready to talk.
