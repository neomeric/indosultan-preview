Who we are

A vertically integrated palm enterprise — from groves to finished goods.

"Quality is the best business plan." We've built our company around that idea — and a decade of long-term partnerships are the proof.

PT Indo Sultan Jaya is a leader in the palm oil and derivatives industry, known for delivering premium-quality products and fostering long-term partnerships with both customers and suppliers.

Our product range spans cooking oil, margarine, fat-shortenings, soap noodles, finished bar soap and palm-oil derivatives — all derived from the finest palm oil and serving both national and international markets.

Our state-of-the-art saponification plant, oil refinery and soap manufacturing facilities in Indonesia proudly serve markets across Asia, Africa, Europe, Oceania and the Americas.
